1. Introduction

This Account Deletion Policy explains how registered stakeholders of the SmartConnex Mobile Application may request deletion of their account and how associated data is handled.

2. Eligibility to Request Deletion

  • Authorized CHA or CFS representatives
  • Primary Contact registered in the SmartConnex system
  • Organization-level administrators

Due to financial and regulatory obligations, deletion requests are subject to verification.

3. How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: In-App Request

Navigate to:
Profile → Settings → Security → Delete Account

Option 2: Email Request

If unable to access the app, send a request to:
Email: [Insert Official Email Address]
Subject: Account Deletion Request – SmartConnex Mobile App

Include:

  • Organization name
  • Registered email ID
  • Contact number
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

4. Verification Process

  • Email ownership verification
  • Organizational authorization confirmation
  • Pending financial obligation check

Processing Time: Up to 7 business days after verification.

5. What Happens After Deletion

  • Login access permanently disabled
  • MPIN revoked
  • Biometric preference removed
  • Wallet access terminated
  • Active sessions invalidated

6. Data Deletion & Retention

6.1 Data Deleted

  • Login credentials
  • MPIN data
  • Biometric preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Session data

6.2 Data Retained (Regulatory Requirement)

Certain financial and operational records may be retained to comply with:

  • Indian GST and tax regulations
  • Financial audit requirements
  • Payment reconciliation laws

Retention period may extend up to 8 years or as required by applicable law. Retained data will not be used for marketing or advertising.

7. Wallet Balance & Financial Settlement

  • Remaining wallet balance must be settled before deletion.
  • Pending payouts must be completed or reconciled.
  • Deletion cannot proceed if financial obligations remain unresolved.

8. Irreversibility

Once deleted, the account cannot be restored. New access requires fresh registration via SmartConnex Web Admin.

9. Suspension Rights

SmartConnex reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts in cases of fraud, regulatory violations, or security threats.
